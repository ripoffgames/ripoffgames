New game called Limax.io added

Limax.io is a combination of Agar.io and Slither.io. As you eat blobs, you gain mass. When you speed up, you leave a "trap" in your wake. This "trap" lasts about 2 seconds and kills anyone who runs into it. The only way to kill someone is by making them run into your trap, not by clashing your body with theirs.
